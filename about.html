Who’s behind this

Hi, I’m Alexander (engineer & spreadsheet tragic). I built BorrowPower to make the early steps of home-loan maths quick and transparent. This site is independent; we don’t recommend specific lenders or products.

What BorrowPower does

  • Borrowing Power: a first-pass estimate based on income, dependants and typical living-cost buffers.
  • Repayment Estimator: Principal & Interest and Interest-Only scenarios with weekly/fortnightly/monthly options.
  • Up-front costs: Stamp duty by state and an indicative LMI estimate to help budget total costs.

These are estimates for learning and planning. They’re not personal financial advice.

How the calculators work

  • Interest rate: default shown on each tool; you can change it.
  • Buffering: borrowing power includes a prudent rate buffer to mimic lender stress-testing.
  • Tax: uses 2025–26 Australian tax settings to convert gross → net where relevant.
  • Living costs: dependants + a baseline living-cost table (not a lender’s HEM) for a realistic range.
  • Repayments: standard amortisation for P&I; IO shows interest-only during IO, then P&I for the remaining term.
  • LMI: indicative only—actual LMI varies by lender, product, LVR and profile.

For full assumptions, see the FAQ and Important Information.

What’s not included (yet)

  • Credit history, lender-specific policies, fees or special rates.
  • Detailed HEM test, bonus-income haircuts, or multi-debt scenarios.
  • Personal advice—BorrowPower is a calculator, not a credit provider or broker.
