About Me

I created HomeLoanMate because, like many of you, I was frustrated by the complexity and constant push to sign up, give my details, or get sales calls from other loan websites.

All I wanted was a simple, honest tool that could quickly tell me my borrowing power or estimate repayments — to help me decide if a property was realistically within reach or just a pipe dream.

That’s exactly what this website is: a no-strings-attached place to get straightforward answers without being pressured or tracked.

There’s no collection of your personal data here. The only “cost” is if you choose to support the site by clicking on affiliate links or checking out relevant ads — which helps keep HomeLoanMate free and available for everyone.

My goal is simple: to give you a solid footing in a confusing world of loans and property buying — because everyone deserves clear, unbiased information.
