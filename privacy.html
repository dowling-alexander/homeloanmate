Privacy Policy

BorrowPower (“we”, “us”) respects your privacy. This page explains what we collect and why.

  • To operate the calculators and improve the site.
  • To display advertising via Google AdSense (if enabled).

We use localStorage to optionally remember your last inputs. You can clear or disable this anytime in your browser.

We may show Google AdSense ads. Google may use cookies and collect data as described in Google’s Privacy & Terms. You can manage ad personalisation in your Google account.
